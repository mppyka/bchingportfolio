Hi, My name is Brian Ching and I'm a comic book artist who's worked for over 2 decades penciling different series for Dark Horse, Marvel, and DC comics. This portfolio will also look at how my style has changed over the course of my career and the process of my work as my pencils are finished with inks and colors by other artists. Click on the images to see the websites I found them from. The captions underneath each one has a link where you can buy the books I've worked on.
